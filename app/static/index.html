How this works

Follow the four steps below, top to bottom. You don't need any technical knowledge — each step tells you exactly what to do.

  1. Upload today's tickets. Add the photos you took of today's tickets.
  2. Extract the data. Press one button and let the tool read the labels for you.
  3. Review the spreadsheet. Download it, fix any colored cells, and save.
  4. Send back your corrections. Upload your saved spreadsheet so the tool can learn.

    What the colors mean

    • White — confident. Nothing to do.
    • Amber — double-check this guess.
    • Red — blank. Please fill this in.

    When you're done, save the file and move on to step 4.
